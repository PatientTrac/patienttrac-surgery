💰 Revenue Optimization

Our AI automatically analyzes documentation complexity and suggests optimal E&M codes (99201-99215). Average practices see 18-23% revenue increase from proper level-of-service coding and reduced downcoding by payers. Real-time MDM calculator ensures compliant upcoding.

Benchmark your E&M distribution against national bariatric surgery averages. Dashboard shows your Level 3 vs Level 4 vs Level 5 visit ratios compared to peers, highlighting $50K-$200K annual revenue opportunities from documentation enhancement.
